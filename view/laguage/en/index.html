About Us

At SIGMA, we strive to provide a balanced view on electric cars and their true environmental impact. Contrary to popular belief, the mass production of batteries for electric vehicles is not as clean as one might think. Extracting natural resources for these batteries has devastating consequences for the environment and the local communities where this activity takes place.

Instead of focusing exclusively on the adoption of electric cars, we promote comprehensive solutions that address environmental challenges more effectively.

Our Services

Research and Analysis

We conduct thorough research on the environmental impact of the production and use of electric cars, highlighting the negative consequences for the environment and local communities.

Public Awareness

We organize awareness campaigns to inform society about the lesser-known effects of electric mobility on the environment and the affected local populations.

Environmental Advocacy

We advocate for stricter policies and regulations in the automotive industry to ensure more sustainable production and use of vehicles, promoting more environmentally friendly alternatives.

Environmental Education

We offer educational programs on sustainable mobility and transportation alternatives that minimize environmental impact, fostering a change in attitude towards urban mobility.
