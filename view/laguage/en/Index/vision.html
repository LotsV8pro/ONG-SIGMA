At SIGMA, we aspire to a future where electric mobility integrates harmoniously with the environment and contributes significantly to global sustainability. Our vision includes:

  • Environmental Balance: We envision a future where electric mobility is in balance with natural ecosystems, minimizing its impact on biodiversity and reducing its carbon footprint.
  • Social Justice: We strive to ensure that the transition to electric mobility benefits all communities, avoiding the exploitation of natural resources and protecting the rights of local populations.
  • Responsible Innovation: We promote technological innovation that prioritizes sustainability and social responsibility, developing electric mobility solutions that are efficient, accessible, and environmentally friendly.
  • Active Participation: We envision a future where all stakeholders, from industry to civil society, actively participate in decision-making related to electric mobility, ensuring an inclusive and collaborative approach.
  • Global Resilience: We aim to build a future where electric mobility contributes to global resilience, mitigating the impacts of climate change and strengthening societies' capacity for adaptation worldwide.

Our vision is one where electric mobility is an integral part of a sustainable and just future for all.
