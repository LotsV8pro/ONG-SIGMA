At SIGMA, our mission is to challenge the prevailing narrative about electric cars and promote a deeper understanding of their environmental impact.
We are committed to:

  1. Inform Truthfully: We provide accurate and objective information about the production and use of electric cars, highlighting both their benefits and environmental challenges.
  2. Educate and Raise Awareness: We aim to increase public awareness about the lesser-known effects of electric mobility on the environment and on local communities affected by resource extraction for battery manufacturing.
  3. Promote Integrated Solutions: We advocate for sustainable mobility solutions that go beyond simply adopting electric cars, including improving public transportation, promoting shared mobility alternatives, and developing innovative technologies that minimize environmental impact.
  4. Advocate for Transparency and Accountability: We demand transparency in the automotive industry and advocate for stricter ethical and environmental standards in the production of electric vehicles and their components.
  5. Foster Constructive Dialogue: We promote open and constructive debate about the future of mobility and the role of electric cars in transitioning to a more sustainable society.

Our mission is to drive positive change in the perception and practice of electric mobility, with the ultimate goal of preserving our planet for future generations.
